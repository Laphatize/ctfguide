CTFLive (beta)

A group based competitive gamemode.

Perfect for classes, clubs, and small hackathons.

Leaderboards

See people's progress in the activity update live. This really puts pressure on participants and gives them an idea on where they are compared to the rest.

Checkpoints

There isn't just one flag. Challenges that support CTFLive are required to have three flags. Participants must find one flag to continue to find the next flag.

Simple to use

No need to code an entire website for your CTF event or pay ridiculous prices for a simple event. It takes less than two minutes to get setup and less than 10 seconds for participants to join. CTFLive supports up to 100 participants. You can donate $4/month to get unlimited participants.

Due to how new this feature is. Many challenges don't support this game format (a lot of them will have to be redesigned to support this gamemode). We expect this page to populate over the coming weeks.
